Kismet

The idea was to create a website that focuses on friendships
with short or long term purposes according to
mutual interests and personalities

User Research

Proto-Persona

It was one of my roles to do proto-persona. I chose a female charcter in her thirties who moved to the city and wants to have friends with mutual interests to do some activites and make connections.

We focused on social reality, personality, and needs to be filled

I designed the face by adobe illustrator, then I used Figma.

Interviews

Our team conducted mutiple interviews to do our research for the
app, then we worked on affinity diagram, empathy map and user insight (Here are some user notes)

My role:
I did some of the interviews and participated in affinity diagram and empathy map

Competitor Analysis

We did business SWOT analysis and features analysis with four apps

My Role:
business analysis was my responsibility in the project. I chose:
- Bumble has friends option
- So Syncd and harmony has personalities survey
- Meetup focuses on activites and mutual interests

Synthesis and Definition

User Persona

We created it by using our research and user insights.

We mentioned her challenges in finding friends that increases with age, in addition to time restrictions according to busy work schedule and mutual personalities.

User Journey

I did this map to clarify the ups and downs of customer along the way.

I showed the overwhelming of apps and the doubts of meeting people in person, but the enthusiasm increases with trial and the mutual personalities that have been matched through survey

My Role:
